Module 35 — Text Gradient Reveal

Words that fill with color on scroll

Large text starts dim. Each word lights up with a vibrant gradient as your scroll reaches it. Three variants: word-by-word, sentence, and per-character.

Variant 1 — Word by word
Variant 2 — Different gradient, longer text
Variant 3 — Character by character
Why it works

Controlled reading pace

Revealing text word-by-word forces visitors to read at the pace you set. The gradient adds visual reward — each word feels like an achievement. Manifesto pages, brand statements, and product intros all hit harder with this scroll-driven reveal.
