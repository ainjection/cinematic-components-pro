Why it works

Stagger creates visual rhythm

When items appear one after another, the eye naturally follows the sequence. This guides attention across the grid without overwhelming the viewer. Feature pages, portfolios, and pricing grids all benefit from staggered reveals. The slight delay between each card creates anticipation.
