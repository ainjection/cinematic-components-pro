Why it works

Numbers earn credibility

Static numbers are easy to skip. Animated counters create a micro-moment of attention — visitors watch the number climb and register the scale. SaaS dashboards, landing pages, and annual reports all benefit from this pattern. The scroll trigger ensures the animation only plays when visible.
